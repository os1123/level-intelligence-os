The highest-value work lives in RFQs, emails, calls, spreadsheets, permit threads, broker notes, project folders, and individual memory. The system makes that judgment searchable, routable, and measurable.

Procore bridge strategy

Procore is the bridge, not the battlefield.

Level Intelligence OS should wrap around Procore first. Procore remains the execution source of truth while the new layer handles intelligence, RFQs, relationship memory, approvals, and executive decisions.

1. ReadReference only the Procore data needed for visibility.No disruption to field or project teams.
2. OverlayAdd signal intake, RFQ command center, relationship memory, and executive brief.Operating rhythm improves without platform migration.
3. MeasureAudit which workflows belong in Level-owned software.Replacement decisions become evidence-based.
4. ReplaceMove modules only where the owned layer is clearly better.Monolith dependence reduces without risky rip-and-replace.
