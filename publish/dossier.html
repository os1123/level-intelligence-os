Dossier summary

The message to Level.

Level Intelligence OS is a private operating layer around how Level finds, qualifies, routes, and converts opportunities. It starts by wrapping around Procore, not replacing it, then compounds value through Level-owned operating memory.

PainJudgment is scattered across RFQs, emails, calls, spreadsheets, permit threads, broker notes, and memory.
PromiseOutcomes, not just code: faster triage, cleaner visibility, and fewer missed follow-ups.
AssetLevel owns the data, domain model, workflow definitions, and generated operating memory.

Implementation stance

Build around the current source of truth.

Procore remains the execution system first. Level Intelligence OS adds the upstream and executive layer: signals, qualification, RFQs, relationship memory, approval routing, and weekly briefs.
